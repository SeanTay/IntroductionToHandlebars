## A table

```handlebars
<table>
	<thead>
		<tr class='invoice-line-item-header'>
			<td class='row-index-key'>{{idLabel}}</td>
			<td>{{productLabel}}</td>
			<td>{{descriptionLabel}}</td>
			<td>{{priceLabel}}</td>
		</tr>
	</thead>
	<tbody>
	{{#each invoiceItem}}
		<tr class='invoice-line-item'>
			<td class='row-index-key'>{{id}}</td>
			<td>{{product}}</td>
			<td>{{description}}</td>
			<td>{{price}}</td>
		</tr>
	{{/each}}
	</tbody>
</table>
```
